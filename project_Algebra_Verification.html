In this project I formalized standard results one might encounter in a first course on Abstract Algebra. You may ask why I did this. Well, I just happened to be interested in formal theorem proving and Algebra, so this seemed like a nice little project.

More concretely, I started off with the axiomatic definitions of Groups and Rings and tried to somehow prove a couple of basic theorems. Notable ones were Lagrange's Theorem and the Homomorphism Theorem from Group Theory, as well as the universal property of the field of fraction and PID's being UFD's in Ring Theory.
